Our Story

Founded in 2020, Nusantara Pastry was born from a love for artisanal baking and the desire to share it with the world. Combining French pastry techniques with the rich flavors of Indonesia, we create sweets that not only please the taste buds but also tell a story.

Every pastry we make is a work of art — handcrafted, baked fresh daily, and served with love.
